The coolest creature

The horse is awesome because horses have a strong sense of hearing, able to pick up sounds in the 55–33,500 hertz range, so they can hear things that humans (who can hear between 20–20,000 hertz) miss. In addition, horses can see very well, with a nearly 360-degree field of vision, and their eyes are larger than those of any land mammal. Foals Can Stand, Run, and Trot Shortly After Birth.
