Travel to Malaysia

  1. Seeing a full size roller coaster in a shopping centre
    Times Square in Kuala Lumpur is a huge multi story shopping centre complete with restaurants, arcades, cinemas and a theme park!
  2. Out of this world snorkelling in Langkawi
    You can snorkel from the beach and swim with hundreds of beautiful fish, some are actually very big – plus reef sharks (they don’t eat people, usually).
  3. Wildlife
    There is wildlife everywhere - you can even see wild monkey’s in the capital city. Staying on an island like Tioman you are likely to see flying foxes, monitor lizards, sea turtles, dwarf deer, plenty of sea life, monkeys and an assortment of bird life. You can also head to Borneo for orang-utans and gibbons.
  4. Weather
    The weather in Malaysia is pretty much always good, even in monsoon season where there is a usually 30 minute downpour sometime in the evening.
